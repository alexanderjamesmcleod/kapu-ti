## Recommended Starter Patterns (4-6 cards)

These are ideal for gameplay - common, useful, and fit the 7-card limit:

### Beginner (3-4 cards)
1. `Ko te [noun]` - "The [thing]"
2. `He [noun] tēnei` - "This is a [thing]"
3. `Kei te [verb] au` - "I am [doing]"
4. `Ko wai koe?` - "Who are you?"

### Intermediate (4-5 cards)
1. `Ko te [noun] tērā` - "That is the [thing]"
2. `He [adjective] te [noun]` - "The [thing] is [adjective]"
3. `Kei hea te [noun]?` - "Where is the [thing]?"
4. `Kei te pēhea koe?` - "How are you?"

### Advanced (5-7 cards)
1. `Kei [location] te [noun]` - "The [thing] is at [place]"
2. `Kāore au e [verb] ki te [noun]` - "I will not [verb] to the [place]"
3. `Me [verb] koe ki te [noun]` - "You should [verb] to the [place]"

## Implementation Notes

1. **Card Generation:** For each pattern, generate a hand that includes:
   - All required cards to complete the pattern
   - 2-3 "distractor" cards (wrong colors/types)

2. **Difficulty Scaling:**
   - Beginner: Show English translation hint
   - Intermediate: Show word type hints only
   - Advanced: Color matching only

3. **Scoring Modifiers:**
   - Correct placement: 10 pts
   - Topic bonus (matches current topic): +5 pts
   - Speed bonus (under 10 seconds): +3 pts
   - Perfect sentence (no mistakes): +10 pts
